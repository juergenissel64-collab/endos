# Code of Conduct

ENDOS development should be professional, respectful and evidence-oriented.

## Expected Conduct

- Treat contributors, reviewers and users with respect.
- Separate critique of ideas from critique of people.
- Make uncertainty transparent rather than hiding it.
- Avoid overstating maturity, evidence or capabilities.
- Maintain a constructive, learning-oriented approach.

## Quality Culture

ENDOS is intended to support better decisions. Its own development should therefore model the same principles: clarity, traceability, plausibility, validation and continuous improvement.
